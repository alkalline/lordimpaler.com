The Band

Biography

  Lord Impaler is a Hellenic melodic black metal act. Unlike most Greek black metal bands, our musical direction has a strong scandinavic flavor. Unlike the majority of contemporary metal bands that succumbed to the triumph of 'fragmentaire', we present a kind of black metal that is rarely met anymore, pure to the tradition and the origin, presented in a modern way and worked with stellar technics.

  Formed in late 1998 in Kastoria, Greece, we proceeded to record and release our first demo The Lord Impaler   in 1999, followed by Twilight Wander   in 2001. Then the band went into hiatus. We returned into action in 2005 and went on to release Promo 2005  . In 2008 we participated in a 4-way split under the title The Ascending Novelty   and in 2009 we released Demo 2009  . In 2011 we released our debut album Admire the Cosmos Black  . It was mixed and mastered at Mølla Lydstudio by Knut M. Valle (Arcturus). Drums were performed by the legendary Hellhammer (Mayhem). In 2013 we released Babylon Whore  , a 2 song EP. It was mixed and mastered by George Stournaras at Infected_Studios. Drums on this recording were performed by George Trakas (Mass Infection).

  These days we are about to release our anniversary EP The Serpent Seal – Το Αρχέγονο Σκότος Επιστρέφει   to celebrate 15 years from the release of our first demo. It was mixed at Thrawnsonic Labs and mastered at Strype Audio by Tom Kvålsvoll . Furthermore, we are adding final touches to our second full length album In Full Regalia  , as we are getting ready to begin recording it.
