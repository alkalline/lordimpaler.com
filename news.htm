April 12, 2010
  As we get closer to the release of our debut album "Admire The Cosmos Black", we feel it is time to give some more information about it. The album contains 10 tracks of approximately 60 minutes of melodic black metal. The song titles are Twelve Thrones Eternal, Celebrating The Elder Spirits, Antichristian Hordes, Homage, Void, The Lord Impaler, Prometheus, Warchant, Hymn To The Nymphe*, Dreamtime Heresy. (* originally written by Daemonia Nymphe).
The album was mixed and mastered at Mølla Lydstudio in Norway, by mr. Knut M. Valle (Arcturus). The drums on this album were performed by the legendary Hellhammer (Mayhem/Arcturus).
We are currently finishing the layout of the album, a mockup preview of the cover is however available. We have also uploaded audio samples of all tracks on our myspace site so you can get a first glimpse of what we have in store...
