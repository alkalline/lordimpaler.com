Babylon Whore

Babylon Whore
Year: 2013
Category: EP

Babylon Whore was recorded between January and March 2013 by Lord Impaler. Drums were recorded at Infected_Studios, performed by George Trakas (Mass Infection).

Babylon Whore was mixed and mastered at Infected_Studios by George Stournaras between March and May 2013.

Tracklisting:
1. Babylon Whore (3:57)
2. The White Dream Of Ziz (5:07)

Line-up:
Tragon - Vocals
Lord Nebulah - Guitars
Phlegethon - Bass
Drums performed by George Trakas (Mass Infection)
