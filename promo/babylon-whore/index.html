LORD IMPALER, Babylon Whore

(2013, E.P. & Split with ATHOS)

LORD IMPALER are proud to unleash yet another blasphemy unto the world. Babylon Whore is planned for release in June 23, 2013 as a digital EP (available throught the band's Bandcamp page) and as a split-CDR with the elite Hellenic act ATHOS.

Tracklisting:
1. Babylon Whore (3:57)
2. The White Dream Of Ziz (5:04)

Babylon Whore was recorded between January and March 2013 by Lord Impaler. Drums were recorded at Infected_Studios, performed by George Trakas (Mass Infection).

Babylon Whore was mixed and mastered at Infected_Studios by George Stournaras between April and May 2013.
