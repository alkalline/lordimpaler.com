The Serpent Seal, Το Αρχέγονο Σκότος Επιστρέφει

Title:
The Serpent Seal, Το Αρχέγονο Σκότος Επιστρέφει
Year:
2014
Category:
EP

The Serpent Seal was recorded by Lord Impaler.
It was mixed at Thrawnsonic Labs and mastered at Strype Audio by Tom Kvålsvoll between July and November 2014.

Line-up:
Tragon - Vocals
Lord Nebulah - Guitars
Phlegethon - Bass
Aenaon - Guitars
Nodens - Drums

Tracklisting:

  1. Dying
  2. Final Gates
  3. Travel Through Abyss
  4. Admire The Cosmos Black
