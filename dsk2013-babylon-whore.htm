Babylon Whore

Title:
Babylon Whore
Year:
2013
Category:
EP & Split

LORD IMPALER are proud to unleash a new blasphemy unto the world. “BABYLON WHORE” is planned for release in June 23, 2013 as a digital EP (available through the official Lord Impaler Bandcamp page) and as a split-CDR with the elite Hellenic black metal act ATHOS.

Babylon Whore was recorded between January and March 2013 by Lord Impaler. Drums were recorded at Infected_Studios, performed by George Trakas (Mass Infection).

Babylon Whore was mixed and mastered at Infected_Studios by George Stournaras between March and May 2013.

Line-up:
Tragon - Vocals
Lord Nebulah - Guitars
Phlegethon - Bass
Drums performed by George Trakas (Mass Infection)

  2. The White Dream Of Ziz
