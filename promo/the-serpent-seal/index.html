The Serpent Seal

Το Αρχέγονο Σκότος Επιστρέφει


We are celebrating our 16 years of existence and 15 years since our first demo release with an anniversary EP. This latest offering is a revival of our demo era, containing songs from our first three demos.

Tracklist:

  1. Dying
    (taken from demo “The Lord Impaler”, 1999)
  2. Final Gates
    (taken from promo “Twilight Wander”, 2001)
  3. Travel Through Abyss
    (also from “Twilight Wander”, 2001)
  4. Admire The Cosmos Black
    (taken from “Promo 2005”)

Key facts:

  • Mixed at Thrawnsonic Labs by Tom Kvålsvoll
  • Mastered at Strype Audio by Tom Kvålsvoll
