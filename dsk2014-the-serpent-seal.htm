The Serpent Seal, Το Αρχέγονο Σκότος Επιστρέφει

Title:
The Serpent Seal, Το Αρχέγονο Σκότος Επιστρέφει
Year:
2014
Category:
EP

LORD IMPALER are celebrating their 16 years of existence and 15 years since their first demo release with an anniversary EP. This latest offering named “The Serpent Seal – Το Αρχέγονο Σκότος Επιστρέφει” is a revival of the band’s demo era, containing songs from their first three demos.

The Serpent Seal was recorded by Lord Impaler.
It was mixed at Thrawnsonic Labs and mastered at Strype Audio by Tom Kvålsvoll between July and November 2014.

Line-up:
Tragon - Vocals
Lord Nebulah - Guitars
Phlegethon - Bass
Aenaon - Guitars
Nodens - Drums

Tracklisting:

  1. Dying
  2. Final Gates
  3. Travel Through Abyss
  4. Admire The Cosmos Black
